Free Fall

Free fall of several letters of snow-like substance

Description

All things in this scene are GPGPU 2018, which is the name of the course and the year we participated in the course. The word "GPGPU" is formed with snow-like substance and is dropped to see its deformation when it crashed on the ground. The other word, "2018", is just a polygon model. The parameters of particles are modified to see more chunky, brittle effect. The idea of this scene comes from the scene of falling letters presented in the paper, A material point method for snow simulation.
